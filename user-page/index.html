About FoodShare

FoodShare is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste and helping communities in need. We connect individuals, restaurants, and organizations with surplus food to people who can benefit from it.

Our mission is to create a sustainable food ecosystem where nothing goes to waste and everyone has access to nutritious meals.
